NBA Teams

The NBA is divided into two conferences, the Eastern Conference and the Western Confernce, each with three divisions. Here are some of the most successful franchises:

Los Angeles Lakers

The Los Angeles Lakers are an American professional basketball team based in Los Angeles. The Lakers compete in the NBA as a member of the league's Western Conference Pacific Division. The Lakers have won 17 NBA championships, tied with the Boston Celtics for the most in NBA history.

Boston Celtics

The Boston Celtics are an American professional basketball team based in Boston. The Celtics compete in the NBA as a member of the league's Eastern Conference Atlantic Division. Founded in 1946 as one of the league's original eight teams, the Celtics play their home games at TD Garden. The Celtics have won 18 championships, the most in NBA history.

Chicago Bulls
