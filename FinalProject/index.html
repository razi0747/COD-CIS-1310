Welcome to the NBA Wiki

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a professional basketball league in North America. The league is composed of 30 teams and is one of the major professional sports leagues in the United States.

The NBA is an active member of USA Basketball, which is recognized by FIBA as the national body for basketball in the United States.
