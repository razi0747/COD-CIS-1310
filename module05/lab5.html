NBA Goat Debate

A lot of people like to discuss the best player in NBA history, personally, I find this debate useless. These players we compare come from different times and eras. That being said, what I like to talk or debate about is top players of all time. For example, top 5, top 10 etc;. This way, you can actually compare current players to current players and old generation to old generation players which makes a lot more sense. My main take is a pretty common one, but still, I believe that Lebron James is the greatest player of the 2010's and early 2020's.
